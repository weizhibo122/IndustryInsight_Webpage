Introduction

It could be difficult for a student to land a job because of the lack of opportunities to practice interview and information about the position. Students may not be able to perform well during interviews without adequate preparation. Industry Insight helps the students to practice interview skills with each other and with GT alumni and inquire valuable information about the position.
